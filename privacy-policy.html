Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07-Aug-2022

IoTDashboard App does not store any user's data remotely, apart from being stored in the Google Firebase account created and managed by the user himself and defined by him within this application. Moreover, IoTDashboard App does not share any personal or non-personal data created by the user except with the Firebase account created and managed by the user himself and defined by him within this application. Therefore the developer is not responsible for the privacy of the user's data in any way.
